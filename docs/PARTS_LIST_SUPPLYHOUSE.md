## üìù Notes

- All brass fittings are **Lead Free** (required for potable water systems)
- Oxygen barrier PEX is **required** for closed-loop hydronic systems to prevent corrosion
- **Thermowells** allow DS18B20 temperature sensors to be inserted into pipe flow without direct water contact
- **Grundfos ALPHA** pumps feature ECM motors with automatic flow adjustment - major upgrade from standard circulators
- **Caleffi QuickSetter** valves enable proper balancing of multi-zone systems
- Several items were returned (marked in order history) - actual spend is lower than totals shown
- Prices reflect 2023-2026 pricing
